Investment Guide

Welcome to Provex Homes! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to invest in assets and grow your portfolio:

  1. Login: Sign in to your account using your credentials.
  2. Fund Your Wallet: Navigate to the "Transfer" section, where you will find three wallet addresses for BTC, USDT TRC20, and ERC20. Choose any of them based on your preference and fund your wallet accordingly.
    • Enter the amount you want to fund.
    • Provide the wallet address if you are funding with USDT, BTC, or ERC20.
    • Wait for network confirmation.
    • Your account balance will be updated after confirmation.
  3. Explore Assets: Head to the dashboard and locate the "Assets" section. Here, you'll find a list of available properties to invest in.
    • Click on the title or image of any property to view its details.
  4. Invest in a Property: On the property details page, scroll down to find the investment form.
    • Enter the amount you wish to invest in the property.
    • Click the "Invest" button.
  5. View Your Investments: After investing, you can check your investment details on the investment page.
    • View the assets you've invested in.
    • See your interest, percentages, and more.

Feel free to explore different properties and diversify your investments. If you have any questions, our support team is here to assist you.
